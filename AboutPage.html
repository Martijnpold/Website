About Me

My name is Martijn Polderman, I am currently 17 years old.

I have been developing since I was 10 and got very interested in making my own games using Game Maker, this was after playing games for years on my Playstation 2 and crappy computer. This means I currently have about 7 years of experience but most of my early work wasn't something you can really call "amazing", this included some recreated games like Mario with a basic upgrade system and 2D Minecraft with random world generation.

Developing started off as a hobby but ended up becoming my job after I branched off towards Lua and finally Java. At first it was never my intention of becoming a freelance developer but after being hired by a random guy on the spigot forums I decided to become a developer as a small job. Currently I work as a freelance developer for many minecraft networks and program anything from minigames to economy API's and gamemodes.

I'm still working on other side-projects like games or free plugins, those can be viewed in the "Projects" tab above. For more info on my programming experience head to the "Experience" tab.

If you want to contact me for any questions feel free to do so using the contact info in the "Contact" tab
